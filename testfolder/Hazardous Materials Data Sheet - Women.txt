Hazardous Materials Data Sheet - Women
Element: Women
Symbol: Wo
Discoverer: Adam
Atomic Mass: Accepted at 53.6kg but known to vary from 40-200kg
Occurrences: Found in large quantities in all urban areas

Physical Properties:
Surface usually covered by paint 
Boils at nothing; freezes without reason 
Melts if given special treatment 
Yields if pressure applied in correct places 
Found in various states from virgin metal to common ore 
Bitter if incorrectly used 

Chemical Properties:
Has a great affinity for gold and other precious metals 
May explode spontaneously without prior warning and for no reason 
Insoluble in liquids, but activity increases greatly in saturation in alcohol 
Most powerful money reducing agent known to man 
Absorbs great quantities of expensive substances 

Common Uses:
Highly ornamental, especially in sports cars 
Suitable for attaching to kitchen sink 
Very effective cleaning agent 

Tests:
Turns green when put beside a better specimen 
Specimen turns pink when discovered in a natural state 

Hazards:
Highly dangerous except when in experienced hands 
Illegal to possess more than one, though several may be obtained in different locations as long as specimens do not come into contact with each other
